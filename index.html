About Us

A cozy family-owned food shop in Phnom Penh, specializing in delicious cooked local Khmer food from lunch till 8:30 PM.

At Vannak Tasty Plates (ចែវណ្ណៈបាយឆ្ងាញ់), we’re passionate about serving authentic Khmer cuisine. Our menu highlights fresh noodle beef soup (kuy teav) and other traditional dishes, all prepared with love in a welcoming atmosphere. Come enjoy the flavors of Cambodia with dine-in, drive-through, or delivery.
